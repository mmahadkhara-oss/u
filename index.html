Who We Are

Architecture as a Language of Living

Founded in 2008 by principal architect Elena Marchetti, FORMA has grown into a celebrated international design studio with studios in New York, Milan, and Dubai. We believe that extraordinary architecture emerges from a deep understanding of people — how they move, gather, rest, and dream.

Our work spans residential estates, cultural institutions, commercial towers, and hospitality destinations. Each project is an opportunity to redefine what a building can be: not just shelter, but a living extension of human aspiration.

Contextual Design Every site tells a story we listen to first
Material Honesty We celebrate the beauty of natural materials
Sustainable Intent Responsible architecture for future generations
Human Scale Spaces designed around the human experience

What We Offer

A Full Spectrum of Design Services

01

Residential Architecture

From urban apartments to countryside estates, we design homes that reflect the unique character, aspirations, and daily rhythms of the people who inhabit them.

02

Commercial & Hospitality

We craft commercial environments — offices, hotels, restaurants, and retail — that are as functional as they are visually compelling, leaving lasting impressions on all who enter.

03

Interior Design

Our interiors are thoughtfully composed narratives — curated materials, bespoke furniture, and art curation that transform architectural shells into deeply personal sanctuaries.

04

Urban Planning

We collaborate with municipalities and developers on master plans that create vibrant, equitable, sustainable communities where civic life can genuinely thrive.

05

Heritage Restoration

We bring historic buildings back to life with interventions that honour the original spirit while integrating modern standards of comfort, safety, and sustainability.

06

3D Visualisation

Photorealistic renders, fly-through animations, and immersive virtual reality experiences that allow clients to inhabit and refine their spaces long before construction begins.

How We Work

Our Design Process

01

Discovery

We begin with deep listening sessions to understand your vision, lifestyle, constraints, and the story you want your space to tell.

02

Concept Design

Our team develops multiple conceptual directions — sketches, mood boards, and spatial diagrams — to explore the full potential of your project.

03

Development

We refine the chosen concept into detailed drawings, 3D visualisations, material specifications, and construction documents ready for build.

04

Delivery

Our site architects oversee construction from groundbreaking to ribbon-cutting, ensuring every detail of the design is faithfully realised.
